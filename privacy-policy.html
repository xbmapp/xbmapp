Privacy Policy for SleepMate

Effective Date: May 6, 2026

Welcome to SleepMate (the "App"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

SleepMate does NOT collect any personally identifiable information (PII). We do not require account registration, and we do not track your real name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

2. Information We Collect Locally (on your device)

The following data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party:

  • Your sign‑in dates (used to show your check‑in streak and statistics).
  • Your favorite audio IDs (to list your saved items).
  • Your audio playback preferences (volume levels, timer settings).
  • Your language preference (based on your device settings).

This data is stored using SharedPreferences and is under your full control. You can clear it at any time by uninstalling the App or clearing the App's data in your device settings.

3. Third‑Party Services (Remote Content)

Our App retrieves audio files, quotes, and other content from a remote server hosted on Cloudflare R2. When you use the App with an internet connection, your device sends a request to fetch these resources. The request includes your IP address and device type (for CDN optimization). This data is processed solely by Cloudflare and is not stored by us. You can read Cloudflare’s privacy policy at https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/.

4. In‑App Purchases (Subscriptions)

If you choose to purchase a subscription (Monthly, Yearly, or Lifetime), the transaction is handled entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We do not receive or store your payment information. Purchase receipts are verified locally on your device to unlock premium features. Your subscription status is stored only on your device and can be restored using the "Restore Purchases" button.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at this URL. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
